Argentina Football Team

The Argentina national football team represents Argentina in international football. It is controlled by the Argentine Football Association (AFA) and is a member of CONMEBOL.

Players

  • Lionel Messi
  • Sergio Agüero
  • Paulo Dybala

History

The team has won two World Cup titles, in 1978 and 1986, and has been runner-up three times.
